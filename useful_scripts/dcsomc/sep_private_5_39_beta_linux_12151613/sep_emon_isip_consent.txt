Intel(R) Software Improvement Program (ISIP)

To improve our software and customer experience, Intel would like to collect
technical information ("Information") about your operating environment and
software usage to include:

   (1) General hardware information (CPU/graphics/memory information, etc.)
   (2) Operating system information (OS used, version, etc.)
   (3) Software tool usage (features used, error information, etc.)

Intel will collect this Information through the use of Google Analytics.
Google Analytics will aggregate the Information with that of other users
and present the aggregated results to Intel without any personal identifiers.
Information collected by Google will be retained by Google under its own data
collection policies (https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245?hl=en)

Information that cannot be linked to an identifiable person may be retained
by Intel as long as it is necessary to support the software.

You may revoke your consent at any time by using --revoke-isip-consent option of the tool.

For more details, please refer to this article:
(https://software.intel.com/en-us/articles/software-improvement-program)
